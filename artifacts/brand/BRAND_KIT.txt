# The Match Signal Brand Kit

## Brand Foundation

Name: The Match Signal

Positioning: Football intelligence for matchday momentum.

Brand promise: Know what matters before the match turns.

Short description: The Match Signal turns fixture data, tactical context, and match pressure into clear previews, live signals, and post-match lessons for football fans.

Audience: Football fans, creators, analysts, Telegram followers, and World Cup watchers who want sharper context before kickoff.

## Typography

Primary UI font: Segoe UI or Aptos.

Fallback stack: `Segoe UI, Aptos, system-ui, sans-serif`.

Usage:

- Headlines: bold, compact, confident
- Captions: short sentences, active voice
- Data labels: uppercase, small, high contrast
- Long reports: plain, readable, no hype

## Voice

Voice attributes:

- Sharp
- Calm
- Useful
- Independent
- Matchday-aware

Rules:

- Say what to watch, not what is guaranteed.
- Use match language: pressure, control, transition, first goal, game state.
- Avoid official affiliation language unless an actual partnership exists.
- Keep public copy away from betting advice, stakes, slips, or guarantees.
- Make the model sound intelligent, not supernatural.
